GENERAL QUESTIONS



Retail Beauty Academy by Unilever is designed to upskill Unilever Beauty Advisors and Customer Development teams with key knowledge including the science behind our products alongside latest Skin & Hair trends. It also equips Beauty Advisors and Supervisors with engaging tools to captivate consumers effectively in-store, ensuring every interaction is both relevant and impactful.

You don’t need to sign up for the courses. Instead, you will be enrolled to the courses by your line manager/ supervisor.

Yes, Retail Beauty Academy is accessible on mobile devices. All you need to do is log in to your account using a web browser on your mobile device.

Yes, your data is secure. We store your data on our Unilever hosting solution.

To log out, you may click on your profile icon at the top right corner of the screen and select “Sign Out” from the dropdown menu.


LANGUAGE AND COUNTRY SUPPORT

Absolutely! Retail Beauty Academy is accessible from any country. You will only have access to the courses based on the market you are assigned to. Ensure you have a stable internet connection for the best experience.


SUPPORT AND COMMUNICATION

You may visit the “Contact Us” tab and send out an email to the point of contact suggested on the page.


PROGRESS TRACKING

You may visit "My Achievement" section to view your progress.

You will see duplicate courses in My Achievements section because each course has been designed in 2 languages, Bahasa and English.

This percentage growth or drop is a comparison of the current timeline’s data versus the previous timeline’s data. eg. Current 7 days modules completed is 8 and previous 7 days modules completed is 10, therefore the percentage drop is 20% down.
